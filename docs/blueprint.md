# **App Name**: Trivandrum Lens

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Cinematic Hero: A scroll-controlled full-screen hero section displaying cinematic WebP image sequences of food, with parallax effects, an overlaid text block (title, subtitle, description), interactive CTA buttons, and a vertical visual navigation indicator on the right side.
- Categorized Menu Display: A beautiful and responsive grid layout for the cafe's menu, categorized by food type, featuring minimal typography and an easy-to-read design suitable for all devices.
- About Trivandrum Café: An informative section highlighting the cafe's commitment to authentic South Indian recipes, traditional preparation methods, and a warm, cozy ambiance, utilizing the cafe exterior image as a background visual.
- Immersive Food Gallery: A responsive photo gallery showcasing high-quality food photography in a grid layout (3 columns desktop, 2 columns tablet, 1 column mobile), featuring subtle hover zoom effects for enhanced visual engagement.
- Visitor Information & Contact: A dedicated section providing essential visitor information including location details, an embedded Google Maps, opening hours, contact number, and a direct link to the Instagram page.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light color scheme.
- Primary color: Deep forest green (#216A2E) symbolizing fresh ingredients and traditional banana leaf presentation, serving as a sophisticated foundational tone.
- Background color: Soft green-beige (#EDF4EF), subtly tinted from the primary hue, providing a warm, inviting, and spacious feel, reminiscent of linen or raw silk.
- Accent color: Vibrant chartreuse (#CBF871) for highlights and calls to action, evoking fresh spices and a natural zest, creating energetic contrast.
- Text color: Deep charcoal (#262626) for clear readability against light backgrounds, maintaining an elegant and refined aesthetic.
- Heading font: 'Playfair' (modern serif) for elegant and high-end cinematic titles, paired with 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for clean, modern body text.
- Minimal, clean line icons, potentially in a subtle metallic or gilded tone to complement the premium aesthetic.
- Apple-style minimalistic design with generous white space, full-screen hero sections, and clean grid layouts for content blocks to ensure an immersive visual experience. Sections are clearly delineated for easy navigation and visual focus.
- Smooth, cinematic scroll-controlled animations for background image transitions and parallax effects in the hero. Subtle steam effects on food visuals, elegant fade transitions, and gentle hover animations for buttons and gallery items.
